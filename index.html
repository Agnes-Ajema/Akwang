About Me

Her story began in Kakuma Refugee Camp, where her childhood lacked the
fun and learning of a good early education program. Seeing how it held the
kids back, She wouldn't accept this as the only option. Akwang aims to create
engaging learning tools and bring virtual classrooms to the camp. Through these
innovative solutions, She dreams of unlocking a world of opportunity for refugee
youth, empowering them to learn and grow alongside any other child.
